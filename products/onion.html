Frequently Asked Questions

White onion powder has a sharper, more pungent flavor and is preferred for aesthetic reasons in light-colored sauces (like mayonnaise). Red onion powder is slightly sweeter and used where color isn't an issue.
Typically, 1 kg of dehydrated onion is equivalent to approximately 8-9 kg of fresh onion. This offers massive savings in transport and storage.
