Common Questions

Is it water soluble?
This is an Air Dried product containing whole fruit solids (including zest/fiber). It disperses in water but does not dissolve 100% clear like a spray-dried juice powder.
Does it contain sugar?
No, our bulk dehydrated powder is unsweetened and purely acidic. We do not add sugar or carriers, making it suitable for clean-label applications.
How should it be stored?
Lemon powder is highly hygroscopic (absorbs moisture). It must be stored in a cool, dry place in airtight packaging. Always close the liner bag tightly after use to prevent caking.
