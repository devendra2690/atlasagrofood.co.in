Common Questions

Is it made from real lemons?
Yes, it is made by spray drying fresh concentrated lemon juice. It contains natural lemon solids along with carriers to maintain powder form.
Does it contain sugar?
Our standard bulk pack is unsweetened. It is pure acidic lemon powder. We can provide sweetened premix versions on request for beverage manufacturers.
How should it be stored?
Lemon powder is highly hygroscopic (absorbs moisture). It must be stored in a cool, dry place in airtight packaging to prevent caking. Always close the liner bag tightly after use.
