Common Questions

Is it edible or for hair?
Our food-grade Amla powder is suitable for both. It is processed in a hygiene-controlled facility safe for consumption, which naturally makes it safe and high-quality for hair applications too.
How is the color?
Properly dehydrated Amla powder has a light tan to brownish color. Very dark brown/black powder usually indicates high oxidation. We ensure controlled drying for optimal color retention.
Is it seedless?
Yes, the stones (seeds) are removed before dehydration. The powder is made 100% from the dried flesh of the fruit.
