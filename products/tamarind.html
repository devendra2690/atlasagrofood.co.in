Common Questions

Is it 100% water soluble?
Yes, being a spray-dried product, it dissolves completely in water without leaving residue, making it ideal for beverages and clear soups.
Does it contain anti-caking agents?
Yes, standard food-grade carriers (like Maltodextrin) and anti-caking agents are used to ensure the powder remains free-flowing and stable, as tamarind is naturally hygroscopic.
How does it compare to tamarind paste?
Powder is shelf-stable (12+ months), easier to handle/weigh, and mess-free compared to sticky paste. It also eliminates the risk of microbiological spoilage common in open paste containers.
