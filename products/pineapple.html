Why Air Dried?

Air drying preserves the **Enzymatic Activity (Bromelain)** better than high-heat processing. It also retains the natural fruit solids, providing a cleaner label (No Carriers needed) compared to spray-dried options.

Common Questions

Is it water soluble?
Not completely. Since it is Dehydrated (Air Dried), it retains natural fruit fiber. It disperses in water but will leave a natural pulpy sediment, which is desirable for smoothies and baking.
Does it contain added sugar?
No, our bulk powder is made from 100% pineapple fruit solids with no added sugar or preservatives. The sweetness is naturally occurring fructose.
Can I use it in cakes?
Yes! It is excellent for baking as it adds flavor and fiber without adding excess liquid, which can alter cake batter consistency. It works great in muffins, cookies, and frostings.
