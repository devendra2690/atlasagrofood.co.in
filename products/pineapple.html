Common Questions

Is it water soluble?
Not completely. Since it is dehydrated (not spray dried), it retains natural fruit fiber. It will disperse in water but will leave some fruit sediment, which is desirable for applications like smoothies and baking.
Does it contain added sugar?
No, our bulk powder is made from 100% pineapple fruit solids with no added sugar or preservatives. The sweetness is naturally occurring fructose.
Can I use it in cakes?
Yes! It is excellent for baking as it adds flavor without adding excess liquid, which can alter cake batter consistency. It works great in muffins, cookies, and frostings.
