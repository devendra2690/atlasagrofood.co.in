Creative Applications

Instant Noodles

The #1 vegetable ingredient for noodle garnish packs. The flakes rehydrate within 2-3 minutes in hot water, providing a satisfying crunch and visual appeal.

Momos & Dumplings

Rehydrated flakes are an excellent, shelf-stable filling base for frozen momos, spring rolls, and gyoza, reducing production prep time and waste.

Soup Seasonings

Cabbage powder provides a sweet, savory "cooked vegetable" base note essential for soup seasonings, broth powders, and vegetable stock cubes.

Common Questions

How long does it take to rehydrate?
In boiling water (like instant noodles), flakes rehydrate in 2-3 minutes. In cold water, it may take 15-20 minutes to regain full texture.
What sizes are available?
We offer standard flake sizes (3x3mm, 5x5mm, 10x10mm) for visible garnish, as well as granules and fine powder for seasonings.
Is it sulfured?
Standard dehydrated cabbage may contain minimal sulfites to preserve color (as per food laws), but we also offer Sulfite-Free options upon request for specific export markets.
