Common Questions

Is there added sugar?
Our standard bulk powder contains carriers (like maltodextrin) to aid drying, but no added table sugar. It relies on the natural fructose of the watermelon for sweetness. Sweetened versions are available upon request.
Does it have seeds?
No, the juice is filtered before spray drying to remove all seeds and pulp, ensuring a completely smooth, water-soluble powder.
What is the shelf life?
12 to 18 months when stored in a cool, dry place. Watermelon powder is hygroscopic, so keeping the bag tightly sealed is crucial to prevent caking.
