Why Air Dried?

Our air drying process gently removes moisture while preserving the **natural fiber and cellular structure** of the fruit. This results in a powder that is 100% pure fruit, unlike spray-dried powders which require carriers.

Common Questions

Is there added sugar?
No, our bulk dehydrated powder relies entirely on the natural fructose of the watermelon. There are no added sugars or sweeteners.
Is it water soluble?
Because this is an Air Dried product, it contains natural fruit fiber. It will disperse in water to create a pulpy beverage, but it will **not** dissolve 100% clear like a spray-dried juice powder.
What is the shelf life?
12 to 18 months when stored in a cool, dry place. Watermelon powder is hygroscopic, so keeping the bag tightly sealed is crucial to prevent caking.
