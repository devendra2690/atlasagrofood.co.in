Common Questions

Does it dissolve completely?
No, because it is dehydrated (not spray dried), it retains natural fruit fiber and seeds. It will add texture and "pulpiness" to beverages, but won't dissolve clear like a juice powder.
Does it contain sugar?
No, our bulk dehydrated powder is 100% strawberry with no added sugar or carriers. The sweetness comes purely from the fruit itself.
Is it Freeze Dried?
This specific product is Air Dried/Dehydrated. We do offer Freeze Dried Strawberry Powder upon request for premium applications requiring lighter color and instant rehydration.
