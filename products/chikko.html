Common Questions

Is it water soluble?
This is a Dehydrated (Air Dried) powder, so it retains natural fruit fiber and pulp. It disperses well but does not dissolve 100% clear like spray-dried powder.
What is the shelf life?
12 months when stored in a cool, dry place. Chikko powder is hygroscopic, so keeping the liner bag tightly sealed is essential to prevent lumping.
Does it contain carriers?
No, our dehydrated Chikko powder is 100% fruit solids with no added carriers (like maltodextrin), making it a cleaner label option.
