Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Natural and Hulled?
Natural seeds have the outer skin intact (brown/golden), offering a nuttier flavor. Hulled seeds have the skin removed (pure white), perfect for tahini and bakery aesthetics.
What is the MOQ?
Minimum Order Quantity typically starts at 1 Metric Ton (1000 kg) for domestic and 1 Container (FCL) for export, but we can discuss LCL shipments.
