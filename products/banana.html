Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Raw and Ripe banana powder? ▼

Raw powder is white/neutral in color and high in resistant starch, making it a great gluten-free flour replacer. Ripe powder is yellowish, sweet, and retains the distinct banana flavor for confectionery.

What is the shelf life? ▼

When stored in a cool, dry place in sealed packaging, the shelf life is typically 12 to 18 months.
