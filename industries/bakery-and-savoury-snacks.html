Common Questions

Are these products heat-stable?

Yes, many of our dehydrated ingredients perform well in baking and snack applications; we can guide based on your specific use-case.

Do you offer bulk packing?

Yes, bulk packing options (e.g., 20kg/25kg) are available for manufacturers and institutional buyers to optimize costs.

Can you support private label?

We can discuss OEM/private label supply for retail packs depending on product and quantity.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Most of our single-ingredient dehydrated vegetables and fruit powders (like Banana Flour) are naturally gluten-free.
