Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about ordering, shipping, and product specifications.

What is your MOQ for bulk orders? ▼
MOQ depends on the product category and packaging. Typically ranges from 100kg to 500kg for exports. For high-value items like fruit powders, MOQs can be lower. Contact us for specifics.
Do you support different mesh sizes? ▼
Yes. We generally supply:
  • Flakes/Kibbled: For soups and ready-to-eat mixes.
  • Granules/Minced: For seasonings.
  • Powder: 60 to 100 mesh for bakery and spice blends.
Do you provide samples? ▼
Yes, we provide samples for quality verification to genuine B2B buyers. Courier charges may apply depending on the location.
What documents do you provide for export? ▼
We provide Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Certificate of Analysis (COA), Certificate of Origin, and Phytosanitary Certificate (if required by the importing country).
