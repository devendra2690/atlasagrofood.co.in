Mesh Size & Grade Guide

Specifying the correct particle size is crucial for product consistency. Whether you need Granules for texture or Fine Powder for instant dispersion, use this guide to order the exact grade for your manufacturing line.

Grade Name Mesh Count Approx. Size (mm) Best Application
Flakes / Kibbled - 5mm - 15mm Soups, Ready Meals, Pizza Topping
Minced - 1mm - 3mm Sauces, Salad Dressings, Salsas
Granules 40 - 60 Mesh 0.2mm - 0.5mm Spice Rubs, Sprinklers, Tablet Compression
Fine Powder 80 - 100 Mesh 0.15mm (Dust) Seasoning Blends, Instant Soups, Marinades

Why Mesh Size Matters

  • Flowability: Coarser mesh (40-60) flows better in automated filling machines than fine powder (100 mesh).
  • Flavor Release: Finer powders release flavor instantly; granules provide a delayed burst.
  • Suspension: Fine powders stay suspended in liquids (sauces), while flakes will float or sink.
