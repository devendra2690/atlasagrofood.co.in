Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Raw and Ripe Banana Powder? ▼

Raw (Green) powder is neutral tasting and starchy, used as a flour replacement. Ripe (Yellow) powder is sweet and aromatic, used for flavor in baby food and drinks.

What packaging do you support? ▼

Standard bulk packing is 10kg or 20kg in HDPE drums with food-grade liners to prevent moisture absorption and lump formation.

Is it suitable for export? ▼

Yes, we export to the Middle East, Europe, and USA with complete documentation including COA, Origin Certificate, and Phytosanitary Certificate.
