Quick answers for procurement and bulk buying.

Which is better for ready meals and seasoning blends?
It depends on solubility and handling needs. Dehydrated works well for many blends; spray dried is chosen for fast dispersion and specific formulations.
Do you supply both formats?
We can support dehydrated powders/flakes/granules and selected spray dried products depending on requirement.
What specs should I share for a quote?
MOQ, mesh/grade, packing size, destination city/country and any compliance needs.
