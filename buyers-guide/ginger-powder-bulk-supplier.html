Ginger Powder Bulk Supplier

Sourcing premium ginger powder requires checking for Pungency (Gingerol), Aroma (Volatile Oil), and Purity (Ash Content). We supply high-potency dehydrated ginger suitable for tea blends, curry masalas, and nutraceutical extraction.

Parameter Standard Export Spec Why It Matters
Volatile Oil > 1.5% Determines the aroma strength (crucial for Chai/Tea).
Total Ash < 6.0% Indicates purity and lack of sand/silica.
Moisture < 10% Prevents mold growth during storage.
Mesh Size 80 - 100 Mesh Fine powder ensures complete extraction/blending.
