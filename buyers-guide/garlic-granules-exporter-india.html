Export FAQs

What mesh sizes do you export? ▼

We export three main grades: Coarse Minced (8-16 Mesh), Granules (24-40 Mesh), and Fine Granules (40-60 Mesh). Custom sieving is available for large contracts.

Do you provide Phytosanitary Certificates? ▼

Yes, every export shipment includes a Phytosanitary Certificate issued by the Indian Plant Quarantine authority, verifying the cargo is pest-free.

How do you handle fumigation? ▼

We perform Methyl Bromide or Phosphine fumigation (as per importer preference) before stuffing the container to ensure zero infestation during transit.
