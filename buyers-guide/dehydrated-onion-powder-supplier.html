Quality Grades

  • A-Grade (Premium): Made from fresh, sorted onions. High bulk density, vibrant color. For retail and top-tier food brands.
  • Commercial Grade: Standard quality for general seasoning mixes where slight color variation is acceptable.
  • Mesh Size: Standard 80-100 Mesh (Fine Powder) or 120 Mesh (Super Fine) available.

Why Source from India?

Indian onions have a higher solid content (13-15%) compared to other origins, resulting in a stronger flavor yield per kg of powder.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does onion powder cake easily? ▼

Yes, it is highly hygroscopic. We use heavy-duty poly liners in our paper bags to prevent moisture ingress. We can also add anti-caking agents (like silica) if required.

What is the standard packing? ▼

Standard export packing is 20kg or 25kg Paper Bags with inner liners. We also offer 14kg packing for lighter bulk density batches.

Can you provide steam sterilized powder? ▼

Yes, we offer steam sterilization to achieve low microbiological counts (Low TPC) suitable for ready-to-eat applications.
