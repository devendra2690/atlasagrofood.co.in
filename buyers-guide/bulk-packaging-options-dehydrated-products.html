Bulk Packaging Options

Choosing the right packaging is essential to prevent moisture absorption and ensure your product arrives in perfect condition. We offer industry-standard options with Food-Grade Inner Poly Liners for all shipments.

Packaging Type Capacity Best For
HDPE Bag (Woven) 25 kg / 50 kg Cost-effective, durable for Spices & Grains.
Multi-Wall Paper Bag 14 kg / 20 kg / 25 kg Export standard for Onion/Garlic (Europe/USA).
Corrugated Carton Box 20 kg / 25 kg Fragile items (Garlic Flakes) & Premium Packing.
Fiber/HDPE Drum 25 kg / 50 kg High-value Fruit Powders & Extracts.

Palletization & Loading

  • Pallets: ISPM-15 Heat Treated Wooden / Plastic Pallets.
  • Stabilization: Heavy-duty shrink wrap + strapping to prevent shifting.
  • Labeling: Weather-proof stickers with Batch details on 4 sides.
  • FCL Efficiency: We optimize stacking to maximize tonnage per container.

Private Label (OEM)

We can pack directly into your branded retail pouches (stand-up, zipper) or jars. MOQ applies for printing cylinders.

Frequently Asked Questions

What bulk pack sizes do you support? ▼

Typically 20kg to 50kg depending on product density. Custom packs (e.g., 5kg or 10kg) can be discussed for volume orders.

Do you support export-ready labeling? ▼

Yes—we ensure all labels meet destination country regulations (FDA, EU compliance) including Batch No, Mfg/Exp Date, and Origin.

Can packaging be customized? ▼

Yes, we can discuss custom pouch sizes, materials (metallized, kraft), and multi-color printing for large orders.
