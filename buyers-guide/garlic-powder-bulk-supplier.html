Quality & Anti-Caking

Garlic powder is highly hygroscopic (absorbs moisture). For industrial use, we offer:

  • Standard: 100% Pure Garlic (Low moisture < 6%).
  • Anti-Caking: With added silica/agents for humid environments.
  • Packaging: Double-poly liners in HDPE bags to seal freshness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does your garlic powder contain anti-caking agents? ▼

We offer both: 100% Natural (Pure) Garlic Powder and versions with anti-caking agents (like silica) for customers in high-humidity regions or using automated dispensers.

How do you ensure batch consistency? ▼

We source garlic from specific regions in Madhya Pradesh and Gujarat known for high solid content and pungency, ensuring a consistent flavor profile year-round.

Can you ship pan-India / export? ▼

Yes, we support both domestic dispatch (via transport) and international export (FCL/LCL) with all necessary phytosanitary documentation.
