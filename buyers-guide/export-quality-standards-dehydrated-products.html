Export Quality Standards

Compliance is the backbone of international trade. Whether you are importing into the EU, USA, or Middle East, our products adhere to strict physical, chemical, and microbiological parameters.

Parameter Category Key Metrics Standard Export Limit
Physical Moisture Content < 6% (Dehydrated Veg) / < 10% (Spices)
Physical Total Ash / Acid Insoluble Ash < 5% / < 1%
Microbiological Total Plate Count (TPC) < 100,000 CFU/g (Standard) or Custom
Microbiological Salmonella / E. Coli Absent in 25g
Sensory Color / Aroma Characteristic (ASTA standards applied)

Documentation Checklist

  • Certificate of Analysis (COA): Batch-specific lab report.
  • Phytosanitary Certificate: Issued by Plant Quarantine Authority.
  • Certificate of Origin: Issued by Chamber of Commerce.
  • Fumigation Certificate: Proof of pest control treatment.

Third-Party Inspections

We welcome pre-shipment inspections by reputed agencies like SGS, Geo-Chem, or Bureau Veritas upon buyer request.

Frequently Asked Questions

What details should I share for an export quote? ▼

Product grade/mesh, monthly volume, packing size (e.g., 20kg paper bag), destination port, and any specific compliance needs (e.g., Low TPC).

Do you support bulk export packaging? ▼

Yes—typical bulk packs are 14kg to 25kg Paper or HDPE bags with inner poly liners to prevent moisture ingress during transit.

Can you share specifications / COA? ▼

Absolutely. We share full product specifications and typical quality parameters at the enquiry stage so you can verify compliance.
