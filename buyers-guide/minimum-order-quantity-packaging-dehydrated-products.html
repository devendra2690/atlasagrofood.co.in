MOQ & Packaging Standards

Understanding packaging specs is crucial for calculating landed costs and ensuring product safety during transit. We offer flexible MOQ and robust export-grade packaging solutions.

Product Category Standard Bulk Pack Liner Spec Typical MOQ
Dehydrated Onion 14kg / 20kg / 25kg Paper Bag Double Poly Liner 500 kg - 1 MT
Dehydrated Garlic 20kg / 25kg Cartoon Box Inner Poly Bag 500 kg
Fruit Powders 10kg / 20kg HDPE Drum Food Grade Liner 100 kg
Spices (Chilli/Turmeric) 25kg / 50kg PP/Jute Bag Standard Liner 500 kg

Logistics & Palletization

  • Pallet Standard: Heat-treated / Fumigated wooden pallets (ISPM-15 compliant).
  • Stabilization: Shrink-wrapped and strapped for stability.
  • Container Load:
    • 20ft FCL: ~8-10 MT (Palletized) / ~12-14 MT (Loose)
    • 40ft FCL: ~18-20 MT (Palletized) / ~24-26 MT (Loose)

Private Label (OEM)

We support retail packaging for brands. Options include Stand-up Pouches, PET Jars, and Printed Laminates (MOQ applies for printing cylinders).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your MOQ for bulk orders? ▼

MOQ depends on the product value. For standard vegetables (Onion/Garlic), it is usually 500kg. For premium fruit powders, we can start from 100kg.

Can I mix different products in one container? ▼

Yes, we support consolidated shipments (LCL or mixed FCL). You can combine Onion, Garlic, and Spices in a single order to optimize freight costs.

Is the packaging moisture-proof? ▼

Yes, all our bulk packs come with a high-gauge food-grade poly liner inside the outer bag/carton specifically to prevent moisture absorption.
